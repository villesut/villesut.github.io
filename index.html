Our History

Coffee Bliss was founded in 2005 by the passionate and dedicated Smith family. Nestled in the heart of our community, Coffee Bliss began as a small, cozy coffee shop with a simple mission: to bring people together over a perfect cup of coffee. Over the years, it has become a beloved local spot, known for its warm atmosphere, friendly service, and delicious brews. The Smith family has always prioritized quality and community, sourcing the finest beans and creating a welcoming space for everyone. Today, Coffee Bliss continues to thrive, serving as a testament to the family’s hard work, love for coffee, and commitment to their customers.
